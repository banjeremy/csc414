About the Game and the Game Makers

SNIPER SMURKS...Some crazy colored smurks all landed on some desolate island and they all happened to have guns that are currently invisible. Your goal... be the last smurk standing!

Jeremy Jones, Aaron Stricklin, Brandon Wolfe, and Lauren Sears are members of the Software Engineering I class at the University of Southern Mississipi class of 2012, except Brandon who is in the class of 2013. Our class was split into teams and taskedwith creating a video game in 8 weeks and this was the result of all our hard work!

Ackknowledgements THREE.js, Blender, easywebsocket, espeak, and jaCob for no reason.
