Instructions


In this game you are fighting against other players online. To win, shoot the other players X number of times.


How to Control your Smurk


Arrow keys are used to move your Smurk forward, backward, right, and left.

Or use 'w' key to move forward, 'a' key to move left, 's' key to move backward, and 'd' key to move right.

Click mouse to shoot.
